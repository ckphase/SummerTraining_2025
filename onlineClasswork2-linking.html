Top 5 Tourist places in India

  • Taj Mahal
  • Red Fort
  • Char Minar
  • Golden Temple
  • Jama Masjid

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi that served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors.

Char Minar

The Charminar is a monument and mosque located in Hyderabad, India. It is a global icon of Hyderabad.

Golden Temple

Also known as Harmandir Sahib, the Golden Temple is the holiest Gurdwara and the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism.

Jama Masjid

The Jama Masjid of Delhi is one of the largest mosques in India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan between 1650 and 1656.
